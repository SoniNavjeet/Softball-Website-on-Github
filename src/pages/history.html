Overview

Softball is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of nine players each. It originated in the United States in the late 19th century and has since grown in popularity worldwide.

Key Events in Softball History

  • 1887: The first recorded game of softball is played in Chicago.
  • 1933: The first women's softball championship is held.
  • 1996: Softball is included in the Summer Olympics for the first time.
  • 2005: The International Softball Federation (ISF) is formed to govern the sport globally.
